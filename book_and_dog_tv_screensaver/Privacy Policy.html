FreeWind Software Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25.05.2025

FreeWind Software is committed to protecting your privacy.

Welcome! This Privacy Policy describes how FreeWind Software (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information through our apps on Roku and Amazon Fire TV platforms (the “Service”). We recognize the importance of protecting personal information, especially when our apps may be used by children and families.

By using our apps, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

Who We Are & Contact Info

Developer: FreeWind Software

Email (for privacy inquiries): help.freewindsoftware@gmail.com

Jurisdiction: Operating internationally without a physical mailing address.

For privacy-related questions, data deletion requests, or concerns, please email us with the subject line: “Privacy Inquiry”.

Data We May Access

We do not collect personal data directly from users through our apps. However, some technical information may be accessed automatically through platforms or third-party services such as:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system
  • Language and region
  • Usage statistics (e.g., which games are played and for how long)
  • IP address (which may be temporarily accessed for ad delivery)

This data may be collected by Roku Analytics, Google Analytics, or ad networks integrated into our apps (e.g., Google Ad Manager, Roku Advertising Framework) to improve app functionality and show relevant ads.

Ads & Third-Party Services

We display ads through third-party advertising partners, such as:

  • Google Ad Manager
  • Roku Advertising Framework

These partners may temporarily access the IP address or device ID in order to serve appropriate ad content. We do not store, retain, or sell this information ourselves.

Children’s Privacy

Some of our apps are suitable for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Any local gameplay information (such as scores or preferences) is stored only on the device and is not transmitted to us.

If we learn that a child under 13 has submitted personal information, we will delete it as soon as possible. Parents can contact us at help.freewindsoftware@gmail.com with any concerns.

Cookies & Analytics

Our apps may use third-party analytics tools, such as Google Analytics, which utilize tracking technologies like cookies or SDKs. These are used to understand general user behavior and improve the Service, but we do not use this data to personally identify any user.

You can opt out of data tracking by contacting us at the email above.

User Rights

If you are located in a jurisdiction with applicable privacy rights (e.g., EU under GDPR, California under CCPA), you may have rights to:

  • Access your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Object to processing
  • Opt out of data sale (we do not sell data)

To make a request, email us at help.freewindsoftware@gmail.com. We will handle your request in accordance with applicable laws.

Data Security & Retention

We do not store or retain any personal data. Any temporary technical data accessed (e.g., for ads) is handled by the respective platforms or ad services. We rely on the security measures of those providers (such as Roku and Google) to protect your information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted here. Continued use of the Service after updates indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.

  • We respect user privacy, including for children
